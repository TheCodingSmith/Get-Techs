About Our Business
GetTechs Pressure Washing is a Southern California based company that focuses our your needs. Customer service is our top priority and we make sure we leave you happy with our work every time. We stand by our service 100% and guarantee your satisfaction.
Equipment
Our pressure washing units have a 3,000 psi capability and 210 degrees of hot water with 5 gallons per minute flow. With our adjustable pressure and temperature settings we can handle even the most delicate surfaces. Our expert pressure washing technicians get the job done quickly and safely. We accomplish your needs by utilizing wide diameter concrete surfacers to evenly clean large areas such as outside patios, parking lots, and driveways. Our technicians come prepared to handle any surface from concrete to store front windows. GetTechs Pressure Washing abides by all EPA Regulations with our large capacity water recovery system which collects waste water and filters it to be properly disposed. Also, we use a wide variety of detergents, soaps and other chemicals, all of which are diluted carefully for each individual job in order to ensure the best results. Our detergents are safe for plants and animals. Runoff water is never an issue as we use a series of pumps and filters to reclaim and recycle water.
